# Components List

## Core Electronics

* ESP32 Development Board
* Arduino Nano
* nRF24L01 Wireless Modules (x2)
* nRF24L01 Adapter Boards (x2)

---

## Power System

* 11.1V LiPo Battery
* LM2596 Buck Converter
* Inline Blade Fuse (15A)
* Power Switch

---

## Actuation

* Cytron Motor Driver (RC PWM Input)
* 2 × DC Gear Motors

---

## Input

* Dual-axis joystick module

---

## Supporting Components

* Electrolytic capacitors (10µF – 470µF)
* Jumper wires
* XT60 connectors
* Perfboard (for mounting electronics)

---

## Notes

Components were selected based on:

* availability
* cost constraints
* compatibility with rapid prototyping

Where possible, off-the-shelf modules were used to reduce development time and complexity.
